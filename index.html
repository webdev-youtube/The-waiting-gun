WAIT FOR AMMO

Bullets accumulate automatically over time. Watch the AMMO counter in the top bar — it keeps rising.

TIP: Buy More Ammo Gain upgrades to fill it faster.

🎚

SET YOUR BURST

Use the [ - ] and [ + ] buttons to set how many bullets fire per shot.

More bullets = more spread and more damage, but costs more ammo.

TIP: Higher X turns a single click into a shotgun blast!

🔫

FIRE!

When the FIRE button turns bright — you have enough ammo. Tap it to shoot!

All active units fire simultaneously toward the enemies.

TIP: Unlock Autofire to shoot automatically.

💰

EARN MONEY

Every enemy you kill drops $money. Tougher enemies and longer games mean bigger rewards.

Spend money in the upgrade tabs at the bottom.

TIP: Bosses are worth 10× normal enemy money!

⬆

BUY UPGRADES

Three upgrade tabs: [+] GENERAL for bullets & damage, [*] UNITS to unlock more soldiers, [#] WALL for defense.

TIP: Unlock new units early — each one fires on every shot!

🏰

DEFEND THE WALL

Enemies march from the right toward your wall on the left. If the wall hits 0 HP — game over.

Difficulty ramps up over time. Bosses spawn randomly. Good luck!

TIP: Watch the wall's color — it turns red when critical!
